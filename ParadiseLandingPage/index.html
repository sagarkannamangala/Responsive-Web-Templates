Events & Activities for Everyone

Snorkeling

This trip will allow you access to some of the best snorkeling in the area. The water is so clear that you will not believe your eyes. Their are vibrant reefs and large schools of fish that are great for your underwater camera.

Fine Dining

The island boasts multiple different restaurants that will give you a truly unforgettable dining experience. Each with their own award winning chef and signature dishes that allow you to choose from a variety of different cuisines.

Nightlife

This place is still hopping after the sun goes down and the daytime water sports are over. Enjoy loud music in a great atmosphere or a relaxing bonfire on the beach where you can sit back and watch the stars.
